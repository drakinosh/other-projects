These are shell scripts that download html files from manga-hosting websites
and runs a python script to rip the relevant images out.
The instructions are contained in the form of comments within the scripts themselves, and
different slightly-altered versions have been made in order to tackle different situations.

The manganame must me identical to the name given to the manga in the URL of the relevant page.
